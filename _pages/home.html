Boston University
Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers

SASE is dedicated to the advancement of Asian heritage scientists and engineers in education and employment so that they can achieve their full career potential. In addition to professional development, SASE also encourages members to contribute to the enhancement of the communities in which they live. SASE membership is open to men and women of all ethnic backgrounds. The Boston University Chapter of Society of Asian Scientists and Engineer began in 2014.

Our Members Work At

From internships to full-time positions, member of BU SASE have secured positions at leading companies worldwide

Our Pillars

Professional Development

Preparing Asian heritage scientists and engineers for success in the global business world through workshops, networking events, and mentorship opportunities.

Diversity & Inclusion

Celebrating diversity on campuses and in the workplace by fostering an inclusive environment where all Asian heritage cultures are represented and valued.

Community Service

Providing opportunities for members to make contributions to their local communities through volunteer work and community engagement initiatives.
