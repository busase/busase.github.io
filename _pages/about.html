About Us

The Boston University Society Asian of Scientists and Engineers (SASE) is a student organization dedicated to fostering a collaborative community for Asian students interested in science, technology, engineering, and research. Founded in 2014, SASE provides opportunities for professional development, networking, and skill-building. The organization encourages interdisciplinary collaboration, mentorship, and engagement with cutting-edge scientific and engineering topics, preparing members for careers in research, industry, and academia while promoting a culture of innovation and scientific curiosity.
