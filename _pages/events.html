Our Events

The Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers (SASE) is committed to empowering its members by hosting a wide range of events that promote professional development, celebrate cultural diversity, and foster meaningful community engagement. Through career workshops, leadership training, community service and cultural programming, we strives to create an inclusive environment where members can grow academically, professionally, and personally.

Conferences

SASE Connect (National Conference)

STEM Connect (previously known as the SASE National Convention) is the largest annual conferences for Asian Americans in the US. Drawing over 3000 professionals and students from around the country, this three-day long convention provides invaluable opportunities to network with industry professionals and students across the nation, attend insightful panels, and engage in a variety of workshops. In 2024, STEM Connect was hosted in Boston, allowing our chapter to send 81 members. Several members secured summer internships, and over 10 received interviews from this opportunity.

Northeast Regional Conference (NERC)

NERC is the other annual conference for Asian-heritage scientists and engineers in the Northeast, offering leadership workshops, career development, and networking opportunities. Nine members from our chapter attended this year, gaining insights from industry leaders, enhancing their professional skills, and building meaningful connections across chapters.
